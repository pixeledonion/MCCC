About

Manorcunningham Community Childcare Centre LTD is a community based not for profit early yearsservice catering for children 2 years and 8 months up to 6 years.

Staff

The centre employs a team of qualified early years educators with expertise, knowledge and experience in early years education and care. Qualifications range from QQl level 5 to Level 8 Early Years Degree in Early Childhood Studies and Practice.

Curriculum

The centre values early childhood as an important and distinct time in the life of a child. We nurture children's happiness, health,confidence and learning through the Aistear Curriculum Framework.

Parents

The centre recognises parents as the primary educators of their child and values parental involvement in the service.

Regulations and Inspections

Manorcunningham Community Childcare Centre is registered with the Child and Family Agency under the Child Care Act 1991 (EYS) 2016. The Pre-School was awarded excellence in all areas of assessment in the department of Education and Skills Education- Focused Inspection Pilot Project 2016. The service has a continuous record of full compliance under Tusla Pre-school regulations inspections.
